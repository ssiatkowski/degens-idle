HALL OF LOVE

You’ve discovered that love and serenity are the keys to life’s true balance. The skills here are purchased using Love Points, which you acquire each time you perform an Infinite Embrace. These skills are entangled forces within three pillars of balance. For every skill you purchase, its entangled counterpart becomes more costly, reinforcing the delicate equilibrium between growth and sacrifice.

In the first pillar, the cost increases by 2.8 times—reflecting the quantum nature of the universe, as 2 * sqrt(2) represents Bell’s Inequality for quantum entangled particle correlation. In the second pillar, the increase is 27 times, symbolizing the 27 dimensions of string theory. The third pillar magnifies the cost by 42 times, an homage to the ultimate answer to life, the universe, and everything.

Choose wisely, for balance is the ultimate lesson of love.
